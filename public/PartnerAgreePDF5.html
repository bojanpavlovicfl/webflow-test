6. Clawback
  1.   2. No further Commission shall be due or payable to the Partner and Plan may Clawback all Commission paid in respect of each and every Connection which:
    1. is Disconnected as a consequence of Fraud by the Partner or the fraud or suspected fraud by the Customer;
    2. is Disconnected as a consequence of Churn and the Churn Percentage exceeds the threshold set out in paragraph 7.4 below;
    3. in the case of Mobile, shows no billed outgoing minutes or data usage for 3 months of the first 6 months following Connection;
    4. is Disconnected due to non-payment or a history of late payment or unacceptable payment methods;

7. Churn
  1. For the purpose of this paragraph 7, the "Churn Percentage" shall be calculated by:
    1. taking the total number of Disconnections during the relevant Quarter and dividing that sum by the Average Base; and
    2. multiplying the result by 100.
  2. When used in paragraph 7.1, "Average Base" for the relevant Quarter means:
    1. the total base of Connections on the first day of the relevant Quarter plus the total base of Connections on the last day of the relevant Quarter;
    2. divided by 2.
  3. In the event that the Partner’s Churn Percentage exceeds fifteen (15.00%) per cent during any Quarter then Plan shall have the right to:
    1. terminate this Agreement by written notice to the Partner on the grounds that a material breach of the Agreement has occurred;
    2. exercise its rights under paragraph 6 above to Clawback any and all Commission paid in respect of any Connections which have been Churned or remain In Contract; and/or

    1. reduce the future payment of any Deferred Commission and Revenue Share Percentage by half until such time as the Churn Percentage is at or below the limit set out in this paragraph 7.4; and/or
